Grab your laptop and join us to stay cool this August while leveling up your skills! ❄️

Git is the backbone of modern software development, yet many people still only scratch the surface of what it can do.

Join us for an afternoon workshop where we’ll dive into common pitfalls, clear up frequently misunderstood concepts, and answer all your burning questions.

Bring your computer and update your mental model! Let's make sure you (and your agents) stop shooting yourselves in the foot!
