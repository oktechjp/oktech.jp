A brand new Osaka meetup for engineers working at the bleeding edge of agentic engineering. A dozen engineers, a couple of hours, somewhere in Osaka, every Saturday morning.

Claude Code, Codex, and the wider agentic toolchain are reshaping how we build software. Agentic Assembly is a small gathering for engineers who'd like to have real discussions with real people rather than just reading another thread about it.

## What we'll discuss

- The past week's news from the agentic space
- Workflows and techniques that actually work day-to-day
- Which tools in the agentic stack are worth adopting
- Emerging best practices for shipping with coding agents
- Applying agentic workflows across different domains
- How we're each adapting to the pace of change

Expect a high-level, peer-to-peer discussion rather than talks or demos. Software development is our main focus, but we want to explore how these patterns play out in other fields too. Come ready to share what you're experimenting with.

## Who it's for

Engineers actively using agentic coding tools (Claude Code, Codex, and similar) in real work. If you're hands-on and curious about how others are doing it, you'll fit right in.
