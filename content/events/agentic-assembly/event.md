A meetup for engineers, vibe coders, and the curious, working at the bleeding edge of agentic engineering. A casual round table discussion every other Saturday morning, somewhere in Osaka.

Claude Code, Codex, and the wider agentic toolchain are reshaping how we build software. Agentic Assembly is a small gathering for engineers who'd like to have real discussions with real people rather than just reading another thread about it.

## What we'll discuss

- Recent news from the agentic space
- Technology, workflows and techniques that actually work day-to-day
- Which tools in the agentic stack are worth adopting
- Emerging best practices for shipping with coding agents
- Applying agentic workflows across different domains
- How we're each adapting to the pace of change

Expect a high-level, peer-to-peer discussion. Software development is our main focus, but we want to explore how these patterns play out in other fields too. Come ready to share what you're experimenting with.

## Who it's for

Users of AI and agentic coding tools (such as Claude Code, Codex, and similar) in real work, or those who are wanting to learn how they are being used. If you're hands-on or curious about how others are doing it, you'll fit right in.
