Beep Boop. Join us for an evening of techy robot topics!

Why are there no robots in our kitchens? [_Rafa_](https://rafafelix.info/) will get into three gaps that are often skipped: safety, capability, and cost in his talk **"Why the Smartest Engineers Can't Get a Robot Into Your Kitchen?"** Drawing from his Trust & Safety stint at TikTok and years of robotics research, he'll share what it actually takes to build robots that can live in your home without breaking your arm. ([→ Thread](https://discord.com/channels/1034792577293094972/1491335027630538792))

And for our second presentation [_Sacha_](https://sachagreif.com/) is back! We've all been wondering if AI is going to eventually take over our jobs, so this time he'll be sharing some preliminary results from this year's **State of Web Dev AI** survey about [AI adoption](https://stateofai.dev/en-US) in web development.

We are looking forward for you to join us. 🤗
